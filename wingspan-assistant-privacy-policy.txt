Privacy Policy for Wingspan Assistant

At Wingspan Assistant, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about users of our app, and complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Information We Collect
When you use Wingspan Assistant, you can add your first name or a nickname to your profile. This information is considered personal data under the GDPR. We do not collect any other personal information about you. We do not collect any sensitive information, such as your email address, phone number, or location.

Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data
We collect and process your personal data with your consent, which you provide by accepting this Privacy Policy and agreeing to the processing of your personal data. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time.

Purposes of Processing Personal Data
We collect your personal data to personalize your experience with Wingspan Assistant. We may also use your information to improve the app and to provide customer support. We do not share your information with third parties.

Recipients of Personal Data
We do not share your personal data with any third parties.

Retention Period for Personal Data
Your personal data will be retained for a maximum of 3 days. 

Data Subject Rights
Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, and erase your personal data. You also have the right to restrict or object to our processing of your personal data, as well as the right to data portability. You can exercise these rights by contacting us at brad.ellison@live.com.

Information Security
We take reasonable measures to protect the security of your information. However, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Complaints
If you have any concerns about how we process your personal data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Transfer of Personal Data Outside the EU/EEA
We do not transfer your personal data to countries outside the EU/EEA.

Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice on the app.

Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, you can contact us at brad.ellison@live.com.

Effective Date
This Privacy Policy is effective as of 26/03/2023.
